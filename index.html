About Me

Welcome! My name is Kayla Quadros and my pronouns are she/her. I'm a student in the MLIS program at IU, and I am specializing in Youth Services.

This will be a career shift for me. I've been a licensed mental health counselor, working with youth and families, for over a decade, but recently decided to make the jump and pursue a career as a children's librarian.

I have an orange tabby named after a cheese. Can you guess which one? (No, it's not Cheddar, but that is a really cute name for a cat 😸). Check out my CATalog page for some cheesy cat photos!

My favorite animal is a raccoon. Those little trash pandas are so adorable, I wish I could have one as a pet! (Yes, I know they don't actually make good pets in real life, sadly). Alas, I must settle for watching videos of the cuddly critters and their silly shenanigans online. Visit my BFFs page to get your daily dose of cuteness with one of my favorite videos. 🦝
